Fidelity Investments has earned Platinum status, the highest distinction awarded, on the inaugural 2026 Where You Work Matters List. The recognition evaluates employer performance using employee-related data from career-focused platforms including LinkedIn and Glassdoor, with a focus on career advancement, compensation, and retention metrics.

The distinction adds to Fidelity's growing list of employer awards and reflects the firm's investment in its workforce of more than 80,000 associates. The company has emphasized its commitment to creating an environment where employees can grow and build long-term careers.

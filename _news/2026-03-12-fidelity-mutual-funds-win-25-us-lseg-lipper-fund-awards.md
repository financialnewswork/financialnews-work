Twenty Fidelity mutual funds have been recognized with a total of 25 U.S. LSEG Lipper Fund Awards for demonstrating strong long-term, risk-adjusted returns. The honored funds span multiple investment categories including equities, fixed income, asset allocation strategies, international offerings, high-income funds, and sector-specific funds.

The awards highlight the breadth of Fidelity's investment management capabilities across a wide range of asset classes and strategies, reinforcing the firm's position as a leading fund manager in the U.S. market.

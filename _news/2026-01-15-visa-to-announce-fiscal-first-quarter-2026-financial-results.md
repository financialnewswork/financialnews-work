Visa has announced it will report fiscal first quarter 2026 financial results on January 29, 2026, after market close. The company will file results with the Securities and Exchange Commission on a Form 8-K and post them on the Visa Investor Relations website at investor.visa.com.

Executive management will host a live audio webcast following the release to discuss financial results and business highlights. Listen-only webcasts and replays will be available for 30 days following the event.

Visa noted it is in its customary quiet period, during which executives will not interact with the investment community.

Bank of America released its first quarter 2026 financial results on April 15, 2026, with earnings documentation, supplemental filings, and investor materials made available through its Investor Relations portal. Chair and CEO Brian Moynihan and CFO Alastair Borthwick conducted an investor conference call at 8:30 a.m. ET to discuss the results.

The bank, which serves nearly 70 million clients through approximately 3,500 retail financial centers and roughly 59 million verified digital users, made call replays available through April 24. Detailed financial materials were also filed with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission.

Lovable, the Sweden-based AI software creation platform, is expanding its use of Stripe to support its rapid growth. The company has adopted Stripe's usage-based billing for its Lovable Cloud and Lovable AI offerings, in addition to using Stripe for payments and subscriptions.

Lovable accepts payments from customers in more than 150 countries across more than 125 local payment methods, with Stripe using AI to determine which payment methods to show for each transaction to maximize conversion. Link, Stripe's consumer wallet, now drives 58% of Lovable's payment volume.

In preparation for its global launch, Lovable also used Stripe Atlas to quickly establish a U.S. entity, demonstrating how Stripe's suite of tools supports startups from incorporation through payment infrastructure at scale.

Royal Caribbean Group and Bank of America have launched the Royal ONE Visa Signature and Royal ONE Plus Visa Signature credit cards, billed as the cruise industry's first tri-branded cards. The cards allow travelers to earn and redeem rewards across Royal Caribbean, Celebrity Cruises, and Silversea.

The Royal ONE card carries no annual fee and offers 3X points on cruise purchases, 2X on groceries and gas including EV charging, and 1X on all other purchases. The Royal ONE Plus card, at $99 annually, provides 4X points on cruise purchases and 2X on groceries, gas, EV charging, air travel, hotels, and dining. Both cards include Visa Signature protections, no foreign transaction fees, and flexible redemption for onboard credits covering dining, excursions, drink packages, and Wi-Fi.

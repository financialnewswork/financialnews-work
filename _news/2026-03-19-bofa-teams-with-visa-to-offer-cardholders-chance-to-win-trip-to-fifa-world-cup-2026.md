Bank of America, the Official Bank Sponsor of FIFA World Cup 2026, has partnered with Visa to offer eligible cardholders a chance to win hospitality packages to World Cup matches. From March 20 through April 20, 2026, every qualifying purchase with a Bank of America Visa credit card automatically enters consumers into the sweepstakes with no minimum spend required.

Three prize packages are available: a Grand Prize trip to the Final Match on July 19 in East Rutherford, New Jersey; a First Prize for the Semi-Final on July 15 in Atlanta; and a Second Prize for the Quarterfinal on July 10 in Los Angeles. Each package includes round-trip airfare for two, four nights' accommodations, match tickets, ground transportation, cultural experiences, FIFA gifts, a $600 Visa Prepaid Card, and a cash award.

Cardholders earn unlimited entries through qualifying purchases. Alternative mail-in entries are available for those who prefer to enter without making purchases. Winners will be randomly selected and notified in May.

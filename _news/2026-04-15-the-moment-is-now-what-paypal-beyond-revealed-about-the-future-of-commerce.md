PayPal held its PayPal Beyond 2026 event in San Francisco, gathering industry leaders to discuss the rapid evolution of commerce driven by artificial intelligence, economic pressures, and geopolitical uncertainty. CEO Enrique Lores emphasized the need for businesses to treat change as an opportunity, highlighting the company's strategy of investing in innovation and deepening merchant partnerships.

The event featured discussions on social commerce and the collapsing gap between product discovery and purchase, with speakers from TikTok and Live Nation sharing insights on engaging consumers earlier in their buying journey. PayPal executives outlined strategies across consumer payments, small business solutions, and emerging agentic commerce opportunities.

PayPal positioned itself as a trusted partner for merchants navigating AI-driven commerce, offering integrated solutions that help businesses reach hundreds of millions of active consumers while maintaining merchant-of-record status. Day two sessions focused on disrupting traditional business models and the impact of automation and AI scaling.

MetLife Foundation has announced new recipients of its Community Impact Grant Program, providing more than $6.5 million to nonprofit organizations around the world addressing essential community needs. The grants support organizations working across financial inclusion, health, and basic community services in markets where MetLife operates.

As part of the global program, the Foundation awarded grants to 26 nonprofit organizations in Asia, with those specific grants detailed in a separate announcement on April 8, 2026. The Community Impact Grant Program reflects MetLife Foundation's broader mission to build a more financially resilient world by supporting nonprofits that serve vulnerable populations.

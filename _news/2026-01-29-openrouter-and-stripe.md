OpenRouter, a platform providing over 5 million developers with access to hundreds of AI models through a single interface, is building on Stripe to support its revenue growth, global expansion, and fraud protection. The company uses Stripe Invoicing for flexible billing, Stripe Tax for automated global tax calculation, and Radar for Fraud Teams to fine-tune fraud controls.

A key challenge for AI products is the constantly changing inference costs as model providers update pricing. Stripe partnered with OpenRouter so developers can route model requests through the platform while Stripe automatically tracks usage, applies pricing, and handles billing, eliminating the need for manual cost tracking.

The integration demonstrates how Stripe's financial infrastructure adapts to the unique billing and compliance requirements of AI marketplace businesses operating at global scale.

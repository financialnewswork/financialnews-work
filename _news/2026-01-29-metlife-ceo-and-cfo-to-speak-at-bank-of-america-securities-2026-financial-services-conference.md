MetLife has announced that President and CEO Michel Khalaf and Executive Vice President and CFO John McCallion will participate in a fireside chat at the Bank of America Securities 2026 Financial Services Conference on February 10, 2026.

The presentation was available via live webcast beginning at 9:40 a.m. Eastern Time, with a replay accessible until February 17, 2026. McCallion also serves as head of MetLife Investment Management.

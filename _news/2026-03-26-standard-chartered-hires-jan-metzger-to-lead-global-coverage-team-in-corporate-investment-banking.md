Standard Chartered has appointed Jan Metzger as Global Head of Coverage Banking for its Corporate and Investment Banking division. Metzger brings two decades of banking experience across Asia, having most recently served as Co-Head of Investment Banking for Asia Pacific at Citi, where he also held the position of Global Co-Head of Technology Investment Banking. His earlier career included roles at Credit Suisse and Deutsche Bank.

Based in Hong Kong, Metzger will report to Roberto Hoornweg, CEO of Corporate and Investment Bank. Hoornweg described him as "a fantastic addition to our team who will help accelerate the momentum that we have gained in our global franchise over the last few years."

The hire continues Standard Chartered's pattern of recruiting senior banking talent to strengthen its corporate and investment banking franchise across its key Asian markets.

MetLife, Inc. (NYSE: MET) has announced plans to hold a combined fourth quarter and full year 2025 earnings and outlook conference call on February 5, 2026, from 9 to 10 a.m. Eastern Time. The call was made available via live audio webcast.

A telephone replay was available from 11:00 a.m. on February 5 through February 12, 2026, with additional replay access through the MetLife Investor Relations webpage.

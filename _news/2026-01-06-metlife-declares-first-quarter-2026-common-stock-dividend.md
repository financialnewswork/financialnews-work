MetLife, Inc. (NYSE: MET) has declared a first quarter 2026 common stock dividend of $0.5675 per share, payable on March 10, 2026, to shareholders of record as of February 6, 2026.

The dividend reflects the insurer's ongoing commitment to returning capital to shareholders as part of its broader financial strategy.

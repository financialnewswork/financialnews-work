Visa has announced it will release fiscal second quarter 2026 financial results on April 28, 2026, after market close. The company will submit results to the Securities and Exchange Commission and publish them on the Visa Investor Relations website.

Executive management will conduct a live audio webcast at 2:00 p.m. Pacific Time to review financial performance and business developments. A 30-day replay will be available afterward at investor.visa.com.

Visa is currently observing its customary quiet period restricting executive engagement with the investment community until earnings become publicly available on April 28.

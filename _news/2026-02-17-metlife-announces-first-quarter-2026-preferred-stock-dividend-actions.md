MetLife, Inc. (NYSE: MET) has declared first quarter 2026 dividends on multiple series of preferred stock, all payable on March 16, 2026, to shareholders of record as of February 27, 2026. The declarations cover Series D (5.875% fixed-to-floating rate) with a semi-annual dividend of $29.375 per share, Series E (5.625%) with a quarterly dividend of $351.5625 per share, and Series F (4.75%) with a quarterly dividend of $296.875 per share.

The company also announced a Series A floating rate preferred stock dividend of $0.31501229 per share, subject to final confirmation that MetLife has met the financial tests specified in the certificate of designations for the Series A preferred stock, with confirmation anticipated on or about March 5, 2026.

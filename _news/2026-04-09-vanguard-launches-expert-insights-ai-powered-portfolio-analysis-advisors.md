Vanguard has launched Expert Insights, an AI-enabled portfolio analysis tool designed to help financial advisors deliver investment guidance at scale. The tool transforms complex portfolio data into actionable, client-ready insights aligned with Vanguard's methodology, putting the expertise of the firm's portfolio analysis specialists directly in advisors' hands.

Vanguard's annual portfolio analysis engagements with advisors have approximately quadrupled over the past six years. Currently in pilot with select advisors, Expert Insights will be embedded within Vanguard's Portfolio Analytics Tool later in 2026, providing on-demand access for pressure testing and improving client portfolios.

Amma Boateng, Managing Director of Financial Advisor Services, said, "When advisors share their portfolios with us, we can work together on a powerful goal: helping create better outcomes for investors." Sid Ratna, Head of Digital and Analytics, added that Vanguard is well-positioned to be "an important partner" in helping advisors streamline their roles using AI technology.

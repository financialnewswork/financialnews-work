Jio Financial Services Limited and Allianz Group have commenced operations of their reinsurance joint venture, Allianz Jio Reinsurance Limited, following final regulatory approval from India's Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority on March 12, 2026. The Mumbai-headquartered venture combines JFSL's local expertise and digital infrastructure with Allianz's global underwriting capabilities.

The partnership is aligned with India's "Insurance for All by 2047" initiative, aiming to strengthen the country's insurance ecosystem. Sonia Rawal has been appointed Chief Executive Officer, bringing 17 years of Asia Pacific risk management experience, including five years at Allianz Reinsurance in Singapore where she managed the India and ASEAN markets.

Executives from both organizations described the launch as a milestone that brings together local market knowledge and global reinsurance expertise to serve one of the world's fastest-growing insurance markets.

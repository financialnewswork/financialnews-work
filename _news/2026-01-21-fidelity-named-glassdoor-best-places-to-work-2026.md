Fidelity Investments has been named to Glassdoor's Best Places to Work 2026 list, a recognition based entirely on anonymous feedback from current and former employees submitted over the past year.

The firm was evaluated across several workplace experience dimensions, including culture and values, benefits, and growth opportunities. Fidelity also secured a top-five ranking on Glassdoor's inaugural Best Places to Work in Consulting, Finance, and Insurance list, underscoring the company's standing in the financial services sector. With more than 78,000 associates, Fidelity continues to invest in building a workplace culture that supports employee development and retention.

MetLife has announced that Ramy Tadros, regional president of MetLife's U.S. Business and head of MetLife Holdings, will participate in a fireside chat at the Raymond James 47th Annual Institutional Investors Conference on March 4, 2026, beginning at 10:25 a.m. Eastern Time.

The presentation was available via live webcast, with a replay accessible until March 11, 2026. Tadros oversees MetLife's U.S. operations and its holdings business segment.

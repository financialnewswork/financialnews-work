Bank of America announced it will release first quarter 2026 financial results on Wednesday, April 15, at approximately 6:45 a.m. ET. An investor conference call will follow at 8:30 a.m. ET. Earnings documentation, supplemental filings, and investor materials will be made available through the company's Investor Relations portal and the SEC website.

The bank, which serves approximately 70 million clients through roughly 3,600 retail financial centers, said call replays will be accessible from noon on April 15 through 11:59 p.m. ET on April 24.

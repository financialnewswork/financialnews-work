Gamma, an AI-powered presentation platform used by 70 million users, has crossed $100 million in annual recurring revenue profitably while building its business on Stripe. The company relies on Stripe Checkout to collect subscription sign-ups and global payments, with Checkout supporting more than 30 languages and using AI to dynamically present the right payment methods to customers.

With most of its revenue coming from outside the United States, Gamma uses Stripe to accept local payment methods including UPI in India and SEPA Debit in Europe alongside traditional cards. Link, Stripe's consumer wallet, now powers more than 40% of Gamma's payment volume, enabling faster checkout for customers.

The partnership allows Gamma to focus on product development while monetizing quickly across markets without building financial infrastructure in-house, illustrating how Stripe's platform supports high-growth AI companies at scale.

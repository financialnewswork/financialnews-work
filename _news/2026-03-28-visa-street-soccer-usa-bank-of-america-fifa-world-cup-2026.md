With 75 days until FIFA World Cup 2026, Visa, Street Soccer USA, and Bank of America have announced expansion of Visa Street Soccer Parks to all 11 U.S. host cities. New parks will open in Boston, Dallas/Fort Worth, Houston, Los Angeles, Miami, Philadelphia, and Seattle/Tacoma, joining existing facilities in San Francisco, Denver, Kansas City, and New York City.

Each park features two professional-grade Bank of America soccer fields, lighting for extended play, learning centers, and flexible gathering spaces. Programming includes youth and adult leagues, after-school academic support, financial education, job readiness programs, and workforce development through partnerships with local organizations.

The initiative also incorporates Visa & Main, a small business engagement program supporting local entrepreneurs in neighborhoods surrounding each park. Visa serves as Official Payment Technology Partner of FIFA World Cup 26, while Bank of America holds the Official Bank Sponsor designation.

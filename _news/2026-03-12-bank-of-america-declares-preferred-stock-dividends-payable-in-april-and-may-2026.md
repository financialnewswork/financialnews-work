Bank of America Corporation's Board of Directors has authorized regular cash dividends on eight series of preferred stock, with payment dates scheduled in April and May 2026. The dividend amounts range from $0.2578125 to $18.1250000 per share or depositary share, depending on the series.

The announcement covers preferred stock series L, HH, NN, OO, PP, RR, TT, and UU. Record dates fall on either April 1 or April 15, with corresponding payment dates between April 24 and May 4. The series include a mix of non-cumulative and fixed-rate reset instruments at varying interest rates.

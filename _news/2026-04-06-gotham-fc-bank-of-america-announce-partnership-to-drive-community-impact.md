Gotham FC has announced a strategic partnership with Bank of America, designating the bank as the club's Official Retail Bank. The collaboration centers on Elevate Play, a community access initiative launched in October 2025 that addresses the "play deficit" among children in New York and New Jersey through school-based programming and reduced barriers to live sporting events.

The initiative will deliver 20 school-based trainings and provide 10,000 youth, educators, and caregivers with complimentary access to 2026 matches. Bank of America will also support free Soccer With Us clinics in underserved areas alongside financial literacy programming and confidence-building workshops.

The partnership extends to custom content, player-led campaigns, in-stadium presence at Sports Illustrated Stadium, and Bank of America Rewards integration offering exclusive fan experiences.

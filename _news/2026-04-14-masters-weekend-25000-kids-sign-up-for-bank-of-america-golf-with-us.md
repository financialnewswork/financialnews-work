Bank of America reported that more than 25,000 youth registered for its Golf with Us program during Masters Weekend, a 160% increase from the prior year. The initiative provides children ages 6-18 access to $5 tee times through free Youth on Course memberships across 97 Bank of America markets. The program is now targeting 250,000 total enrollments by June 15, 2026.

Professional golfer Rory McIlroy contributed $500,000 to Youth on Course to fund approximately 70,000 golf rounds. Enhanced 2026 benefits include discounted golf simulator rentals and complimentary PGA Pro lessons. Bank of America also serves as a Champion Partner of the Masters Tournament.
